# Laboratory for Evolutionary and Ecological Immunology

**Faculty of Science, Charles University, Prague**

We are a research group studying comparative animal immunology, evolutionary
immunogenetics, and functionally relevant physiological variation in the
ecological context, with a primary focus on non-model bird species
(passerines and parrots).

## Research areas

- 🧬 **Evolutionary immunogenetics** — using evolutionary concepts to predict
  molecular phenotypes underlying disease resistance
- 🦜 **Avian immunology** — immunological variation across passerines and
  parrots (Psittacopasserae)
- 🔬 **Multi-level analysis** — integrating transcriptomics, proteomics,
  microbiota, cellular and organismal biology
- 🌍 **Environmental impacts** — effects of pollutants and human activity
  on animal health
